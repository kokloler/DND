Creating a character: You will be doing this together with me, in whatever app you see fit.
Privately message me so we can meet up and make a character.
You will be allowed to use any official rulesets to make your character.

The amount of characters is dependend of the level of your highest level character

1+ = 2 characters
5+ = 3
10+ = 4
15+ = 5
20+ = 6

Dead characters do NOT count towards this count.

There will be a character database ON THIS WEBSITE that will include all the characters
(dead or alive) that have been made for this game.
